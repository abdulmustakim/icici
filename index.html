FAQ

Questions users usually ask

Is this the official Amazon Pay ICICI website?

No. This is a demo/informational landing page. Always apply only through official Amazon or ICICI Bank pages.

Can I collect PAN or OTP on this website?

No. That would be unsafe and may look like phishing. Redirect users to the official application page instead.

Is approval guaranteed?

No. Credit-card approval depends on ICICI Bank’s internal policy, eligibility, income, credit history, KYC, and other checks.

How should I use a credit card safely?

Pay the full bill before the due date, keep usage low, avoid cash withdrawal, and never share OTP/CVV/password.
